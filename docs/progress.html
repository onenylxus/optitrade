01 — Overview

Three Core Capabilities

OptiTrade Copilot addresses the fragmented information environment faced by professional traders — combining personalized visualization, verifiable AI reasoning, and position-aware alerts in a single integrated platform.

CAPABILITY 01
⊞

Dynamic Widget Canvas

A drag-and-drop workspace for charts, portfolio analytics, and AI-enhanced news. Users configure personalized views of strategy-relevant signals with saved layout profiles.

Charts Portfolio News Feed react-grid-layout
CAPABILITY 02
◈

Context-Aware AI Chat

Conversational AI whose responses are grounded in timestamped context cards exported from on-screen widgets — every answer is traceable to real data, eliminating hallucination risk.

RAG LLM Context Cards Provenance
CAPABILITY 03
◎

Real-Time Alerts & Monitoring

Near-real-time notifications driven by user-defined rules tied to technical indicators, portfolio thresholds, and news risk signals — delivering position-aware, high-precision alerts.

Celery / Redis SendGrid RSI / MACD WebSocket
