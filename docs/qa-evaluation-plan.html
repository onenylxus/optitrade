Six AI surfaces, two gateways

05 — Risks the panel will probe

Five named risks, all mitigated

These are the most likely QA questions and the project's standing answers. Each is referenced from docs/ai-usage-analysis.md.

High

Single-judge bias ≈ 30%

KRAFTON AI (2025) measured ~30% LLM-as-Judge bias on Chatbot Arena. Project uses two judges and reports Cohen's κ — never quotes a single-judge number as ground truth.

Med

Length bias on judge

Dubois et al. (2024) show judges prefer longer answers. Project reports median answer length per set and flags if judge preference correlates with length.

Med

Eval-bait contamination

The 30 bait prompts must not appear in any system-prompt dev log or doc. Held out by the team; CI grep rule enforces it.

Low

Nanobot WS unavailable

Local fake_nanobot.py test backend decouples the harness from the digital-ocean droplet. Eval never depends on it.

Low

OpenRouter flakiness

Per-prompt max_retries=1; nightly job is retryable; 429s counted separately. No live OpenRouter in CI.
